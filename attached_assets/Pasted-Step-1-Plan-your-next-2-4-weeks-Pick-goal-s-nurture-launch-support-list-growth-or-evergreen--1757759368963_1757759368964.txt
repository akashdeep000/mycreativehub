Step 1 - Plan your next 2-4 weeks
Pick goal(s): nurture, launch support, list growth, or evergreen.
Choose channels: IG feed/Reels, Stories, TikTok, YouTube Shorts, Email, Blog, Pinterest.
Decide timeline (e.g. 3 IG posts, 2 Reels, 1 email per week).
List 3-5 content pillar themes you’ll repeat (e.g., Tips, Behind-the-Scenes, Testimonials, Offers). Use Content Pillars within Content Creation System.
Use Time Blocking Calendar: plug in key dates (promos, holidays, launches). Done when: you have a filled in calendar and a target number of assets to make.

Step 2 - Block your batching time
Take Action! Pick 1-2 batching sessions this week for any given task within your Time Blocking Planner and get to work. 
Set a Quick Start Timer on your dashboard and turn off distractions. Done when: your time is blocked and your workspace/files are ready.

Step 4 - Plan content
Brain dump 10-20 post ideas tied to your content pillars.
For each idea, jot 3-5 bullet points (what to show/say).
Map ideas to formats (Carousel, Reel, Story, Email). Done when: you have enough outlines to hit your batching target for 2-4 weeks.

Step 5 - Produce visuals & videos (batch)
Record all clips in one go (same setup, different outfits/angles), OR film as you go, bank content throughout the week and batch the editing. 
Design carousels/covers/thumbnails using your template packs.
Export in correct sizes and store in organised desktop folder. Done when: all visuals/clips for this batch are exported and named properly.

Step 6 - Write captions & email copy
For each asset, write:
Hook → 3-5 value lines → CTA.
Add 3-5 relevant hashtags (if used), alt text, and any links. Done when: every asset has its caption/copy, CTA, and accessibility text.

Step 7 - Upload/Schedule
Load posts into your scheduler (or native app).
Set dates/times; add first comments/links if needed.
Final checks:
Spelling/brand voice, audio levels, CTAs, alt text/captions on.
Hit Schedule. Done when: everything is queued and ready to go.

Step 8 - Publish day checklist (super short)
Reply to early comments/DMs in the first hour.
Add links to Stories/Link-in-bio as needed.
